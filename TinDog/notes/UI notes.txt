*NOTES*
1. Hierarchy rule
	Always use the heiarchy rule.
	the bigger the better.
	Don't use the same color in the whole page.
	Use color according to the importance.
2. Layout
	In your design the length of the characters per line must be 40 to 60.
3. Alignment
	The alignment in the text must start from the same line.
	Reduce the number of alignment points.
4. White Space
	By adding white space around each elements on the screen, it creates the design minimalist and elevate it.
5. Audience
	Always design according to the targeted audience.
