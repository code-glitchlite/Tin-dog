serif fonts:
they are used to define seriousness, authorative and some old style
letter head for a legal company or magazine
four types of serif fonts:
1. old style: used for older and aged
2. Transitional: used for middle aged 
3. Modern: used for modern
4. slab-serif: typewriter

Moods according to fonts:
1. Serif: Traditional Stable Respectable
2. Sans-Serif: Sensible Simple Straightforward (mainly used for body text)
	a. Humanist typeface, Gill-Sans, Tahona, Verdana: it is highly readable.
3. Script: Personal Creative Elegant
4. Display: Friendly Loud Amusing
5. Modern: Stylish Chic Smart

*NOTE* 
Always two fonts in one design.
